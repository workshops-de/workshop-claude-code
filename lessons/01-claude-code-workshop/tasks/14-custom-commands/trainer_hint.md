## Learning goals

- **Skills:** authoring custom commands; parameterizing a command with arguments; organizing team
  commands.
- **Concepts:** commands (you trigger) vs. skills (agent triggers); reusable prompts.
- **Takeaways:** codify frequent requests; make them consistent and shareable.

## Facilitation notes

- Short task by design — a good pace-recovery point if the group is running behind after Skills.
- The command-vs-skill distinction trips people up; have 2-3 concrete examples ready (e.g. "commit
  message" = command, "add a feature the established way" = skill).

## Time estimate

~25 minutes.
