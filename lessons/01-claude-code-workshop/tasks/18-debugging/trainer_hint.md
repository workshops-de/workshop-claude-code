## Learning goals

- **Skills:** providing debugging evidence; guiding hypothesis-driven investigation; verifying
  fixes.
- **Concepts:** root cause vs. symptom; reproductions; observation in the agent loop.
- **Takeaways:** debugging quality tracks evidence quality; fix the cause and prove it.

## Facilitation notes

- If participants don't have a natural bug handy, suggest a deliberate one (e.g. remove a
  uniqueness check) so the exercise stays concrete.
- The rubber-duck step is easy to skip — call it out as a technique worth trying even when they
  already know the fix.

## Time estimate

~30 minutes.
