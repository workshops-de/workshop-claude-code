## Going further — composition & distribution

- New custom commands are authored as **skills**; legacy commands live in `.claude/commands/`.
- **Plugins** bundle commands, skills, hooks, and MCP servers for team-wide distribution through a
  marketplace.
- Compose commands with project context and **arguments** to build repeatable, parameterized
  workflows.

Try turning one of your commands into a small plugin others on your team could install.
